My Hometown

Xuzhou

Xuzhou City, referred to as "Xu", formerly known as Pengcheng, is a prefecture-level city under the jurisdiction of Jiangsu Province, a provincial sub-central city, a national historical and cultural city approved by the State Council, a national comprehensive transportation hub, and a central city of Huaihai Economic Zone.

By the end of 2022, Xuzhou has 5 municipal districts, 3 counties, and 2 county-level cities, with a total area of 11,765 square kilometers and a permanent population of 9,018,500.

Xuzhou City is located in the southeast of the North China Plain, northwest of Jiangsu, the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal passes through, the Longhai Railway, the Beijing-Shanghai Railway two major trunk lines converge here, known as the "thoroughfare of the five provinces". Xuzhou is an important gateway city in East China, an important economic, scientific and educational, cultural, financial, medical and foreign trade center in East China, an important node city of the national "Belt and Road", an innovation demonstration area for the national sustainable development agenda, a sub-central city in the north wing of the Yangtze River Delta, and a core city in the Xuzhou metropolitan area, and has the reputation of "China's construction machinery capital".

At the end of the primitive society, Emperor Yao Peng Zu built the Great Peng Kingdom, which was the earliest city in Jiangsu. Historically, Xuzhou is one of the nine states in China, and since ancient times, it has been the key to the north, the gateway to the south, the place where soldiers must fight and the center of businessmen, and it is also the political, economic and cultural center of the Huaihai region.

Xuzhou has a history of more than 6,000 years of civilization and 2,600 years of city history, and is the hometown of emperors. There is a saying that "the emperor of the Nine Dynasties is from Xuzhou". Xuzhou is the birthplace of the Han and Han cultures, known as "the hometown of Peng Zu, the hometown of Liu Bang, and the former capital of Xiang Yu", and is also known as the "Oriental Athens" because of its large number of cultural heritage, places of interest and profound historical heritage.

Xuzhou City belongs to the temperate monsoon climate, the four seasons are distinct, is a scenic tourist city, there are Yunlong Lake, Yunlong Mountain, Pengzu Garden, Hubu Mountain, Pan'an Lake and other tourist attractions, there are Peng Zu, Liu Bang, Sun Quan, Liu Yu, Li Yu and other historical celebrities. It has won the titles of National Civilized City, United Nations Habitat Award, and National Innovative City.
